We implemented an exponential curve based directly on Clash of Clans scaling. Tier 1 upgrades (Basic Centrifugal Pumps) take 5 seconds. Tier 5 Upgrades (Catalytic Cracking Pillars) take 14 days and cost $25,000,000 in Capital.

### Bridging Sunk Cost with Heat Exchanger Networks (HENs)

By the time a user gets to Tier 5, they've spent 2 months balancing Bernoulli’s equation and finding the Sweet Spot of the Inverted parabola $Cost = a(Power)^2 + b$. Since they spent weeks getting their PID controllers perfectly calibrated, abandoning the plant causes immense psychological pain.

### Loss Aversion & Fouling Deactivation

If a user is offline for 48 hours, hidden durability meters trip. Ethylene polymerization causes carbon fouling. Output drops by 40%. The resulting red numbers hit the user's deep 'Loss Aversion' centers. They are now actively compelled to return to repair the plant, thus cementing our retention cycle.
